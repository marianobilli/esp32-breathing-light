How the pins of the Oled Board with controls connect to the ESP32 pins

## ESP32 PIN : OLED Board PIN

21: CON (Confirm Button)
39: SDA
40: SCL
35: PSH (Rotary control PUSH button)
36: TRA (Rotary control A)
37: TRB (Rotary control B)
38: BAK (Back button)

## Led srip

GPIO 1

## PCM5100/1/2 DAC Module (I2S)

2: BCK
42: DIN
41: LCK
